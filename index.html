Key Questions & Insights

Detailed insights and answers to common queries

How is UIUC's Impact Ranking calculated?

The ranking combines research influence, outreach outcomes, and SDG-aligned institutional impact metrics.

What does the UIUC SDG Score represent?

It is a composite score reflecting university performance across sustainability goals, weighted by methodology.

Why compare UIUC funding with other U of I campuses?

It shows relative investment scale across the system and helps identify where UIUC leads or needs targeted support.

Key Insights for UIUC

Recommendations for advancing UIUC's impact in the Times Higher Education Impact Rankings

🎯 Strengths

UIUC excels in SDG 7 (Clean Energy), SDG 4 (Quality Education), and SDG 9 (Innovation). These research areas showcase the university's capacity for high-impact sustainability research.

📈 Growth Opportunities

Consider strategic investments in SDG 6 (Clean Water), SDG 12 (Consumption), and SDG 15 (Life on Land) to diversify impact areas and improve the overall ranking.

🤝 Strategic Partnerships

Leverage existing partnerships and establish new collaborations in areas where peer institutions lead. Cross-institutional research can strengthen UIUC's competitiveness.

🔬 Research Focus

Align departmental research initiatives with the 17 SDGs. The greater the proportion of research addressing SDGs, the higher the impact score and ranking advancement.
