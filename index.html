FAQ

Common questions about impact rankings, scores, and funding

How is UIUC's Impact Ranking calculated?

The ranking combines research influence, outreach outcomes, and SDG-aligned institutional impact metrics.

What does the UIUC SDG Score represent?

It is a composite score reflecting university performance across sustainability goals, weighted by methodology.

Why compare UIUC funding with other U of I campuses?

It shows relative investment scale across the system and helps identify where UIUC leads or needs targeted support.
